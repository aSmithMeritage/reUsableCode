Bluewood

Bluewood is located in the beautiful city of Celina off of N Preston Rd. Bluewood will be a fully amenitized master-planned community and a part of Celina ISD. Residents will enjoy small town living and being just a short drive to high-end shopping locations and entertainment. Every home we build helps showcase our commitment to providing incredibly energy-efficient homes that let you spend your money on better things than utility bills.
